Featured Adventures

Los Dientes del Diablo

Difficulty: Extreme Duration: 6 hours

Experience the ultimate adrenaline rush on our most challenging rapids! Los Dientes del Diablo (The Devil's Teeth) features Class V rapids that will test even experienced rafters. Navigate through narrow canyon walls, massive boulders, and heart-stopping drops. This trip is recommended only for those with previous rafting experience and excellent swimming abilities.

  • Class V rapids with 15+ major drops
  • Stunning canyon scenery and wildlife viewing
  • Professional safety equipment and expert guides
  • Lunch included at riverside camp

Big Mallard Family Adventure

Difficulty: Moderate Duration: 4 hours

Perfect for families and first-time rafters! The Big Mallard run offers exciting Class II-III rapids with plenty of calm stretches for swimming and enjoying nature. Our guides will teach you paddling techniques while sharing fascinating stories about the local ecosystem and wildlife. This trip strikes the perfect balance between adventure and relaxation.

  • Family-friendly Class II-III rapids
  • Swimming opportunities in calm pools
  • Wildlife spotting including eagles and otters
  • Snacks and drinks provided

Half Day Thrill Ride

Difficulty: Intermediate Duration: 3 hours

Get your adrenaline fix in just half a day! This action-packed adventure features continuous Class III-IV rapids with technical maneuvers and big waves. Perfect for those with some rafting experience who want maximum excitement in minimum time. The compact format makes it ideal for busy schedules or as an add-on to your vacation.

  • Non-stop Class III-IV action
  • Technical rapids and big wave trains
  • Compact adventure perfect for busy schedules
  • Equipment and safety briefing included
